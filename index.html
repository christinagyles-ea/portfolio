Case studies

Designing for water abstraction and impoundment licenses

Discovery to Alpha project for Environment Agency

My Environment Agency multidisciplinary agile team, was tasked with working closely with the operational and functional teams to redesign the water abstraction licencing service. This was whilst ensuring continued current operations and in the context of a large scale policy change.

As the Senior User Experience Designer in the team, I was responsible for creating and overseeing the interaction and service design for the service. I worked closely with the designers of connecting services and programmes to coordinate a strategic cross-services design.

Designing for bespoke waste permits

Beta to Beta live Project for Department for Environment Food and Rural Affairs (DEFRA)

‘Apply for a standard rules waste permit’ was an Environment Agency project, being delivered by DEFRA. It was a private Beta service that enabled users to follow a template of ‘standard rules’ of permitting for set activities, to create a simple to follow and quick to apply for ‘standard rules’ permit. This was initially a paper based journey that was now available online and through assisted digital.

As the Senior Interaction Designer in the team, I was responsible for creating and overseeing the interaction and service design for the service. I worked closely with the designers of connecting services and programmes to coordinate a strategic cross-services design.

Designing for the Get flood warnings service

Alpha project for Department for Environment Food and Rural Affairs (DEFRA)

Flooding is a large area of work for the Environment Agency, as such the Flood programme is a complex landscape of connecting services, with a diverse range of users.

The Warning Improvement Programme which oversees the flood warnings service, is where users can sign up to warnings for their chosen locations. The project was in Alpha when I joined. I led on service and interaction design within the multidisciplinary agile team through to beta.
